Pop Up Games

Company Information

Pop Up Games is a rental service committed to bringing entertainment to your event. We have hourly and daily rates. The games are all portable and easy to set up

Event ideas are: Graduations, Weddings, Birthday parties, Tailgating, Fundraising, Team Building events, and many more.

We are located on Simpson campus in the library and arrangements can be made in advance to pick up games at your convenience.

Company History

The company was a creation of Simpson College students involved in an entrepreneurial business class that focuses on learning the background of how to start a business. One of the concepts learned in the class is developing a business model canvas. The group of students request supplies to be donated by local business owners in Indianola. The students also compete in a pitch contest to win prize money to assist in start up costs.

The goals of the students and the company is to continue to develop the business plan and promote the business.
